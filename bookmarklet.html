Bookie - Cross-browser Cookie Handling

Bookie is a tool that lets you view and manipulate the cookies on a domain.

You will only see cookies that are on the same domain as the site you are currently on.

Add the link below, "Bookie", as a bookmark. You can add these as toolbar buttons but the process is slightly different on each browser.

      Firefox/Chrome/Safari: Just drag the link to your toolbar.
      Internet Explorer(all): Right-click on the link and choose "Add to Favorites". In the Add Favorite dialog window, select the Links folder under Favorites.
**You may need to make your Links toolbar visible if it isn't already.
